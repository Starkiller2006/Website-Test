About Us

20 years in the industry, all of it in your corner

CAS Chemical LLC was built on a simple belief: that sourcing raw cosmetic ingredients should feel like working with a trusted partner — not navigating a faceless catalogue. With over two decades of hands-on experience in the cosmetic supply industry, we've seen every challenge a formulator, brand, or buyer can face. And we built CAS Chemical to solve them.

We don't just sell ingredients. We make ourselves an extension of your buying team — understanding your timelines, your formulation needs, and your business goals so we can help you move faster, smarter, and with greater confidence at every step.

Your success is our success. That's not a tagline — it's how we've operated for over 20 years, and it's the standard we hold ourselves to every single day.
